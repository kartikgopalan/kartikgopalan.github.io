Just Passing By America
Kartik Gopalan

Q : How do you eat a mountain?
A : Bit by bit.

Here's the story of my 12 day journey from New York to San Francisco in September of 1999, when I was a grad student with plenty of time but little money. As I started writing, I realized  the account is going to be so huge that none of the hapless readers is going to have the patience to finish it all in one sitting. So I've written it in 12 parts to hopefully mitigate any indigestions!

1. Starting Off
2. Mad New York - Sleepy Pennsylvania - Gates to Chicago.
3. Chicago's Cows and Sleepy Madison
4. Cowboy Towns : Something for you to remember us by!
5. South Dakota : Fossills, Presidents and Gambling
6. Welcome to Wyoming : Like No Other Place On Earth!
7. Yellowstone :  Heaven on Earth!
8. Hiking at Grand Teton
9. Mormon Land : Salt Lake City
10.  Canyons and Rendezvous with Las Vegas!!
11.  Mojavi Desert, "All Rightie!", Santa Barbara
12. Welcome to the "You can never leave" California.
